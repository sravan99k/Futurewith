### Pricing Psychology Tips

1. **Price Anchoring**
   - Present higher option first
   - Use three pricing tiers
   - Highlight recommended option

2. **Value Communication**
   - Lead with benefits, not features
   - Quantify value when possible
   - Use comparisons to higher-cost alternatives

3. **Payment Terms Impact**
   - Net 30: Standard payment terms
   - Net 15: 2% discount for early payment
   - Net 7: 5% discount for immediate payment
   - 50/50 split: 50% upfront, 50% on completion

## Contract Essentials

### Key Contract Elements

#### 1. Scope of Work

```
The Freelancer agrees to provide the following services:
- [Specific deliverable 1]
- [Specific deliverable 2]
- [Specific deliverable 3]

The Freelancer will NOT provide:
- [Excluded service 1]
- [Excluded service 2]
```

#### 2. Timeline and Milestones

```
Project Start Date: [Date]
Project Completion Date: [Date]

Key Milestones:
- [Milestone 1]: [Date]
- [Milestone 2]: [Date]
- Final Delivery: [Date]
```

#### 3. Payment Terms

```
Total Project Cost: $[Amount]
Payment Schedule:
- 50% deposit due upon signing: $[Amount]
- 50% final payment due upon completion: $[Amount]

Payment Terms: Net 15 days from invoice date
Late Payment: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts
```

#### 4. Intellectual Property

```
Work for Hire: All work product shall be "work made for hire"
Client Ownership: Upon final payment, client owns all rights to deliverables
Freelancer Portfolio: Freelancer may include work in portfolio after client approval
```

#### 5. Revision Policy

```
Included Revisions: Up to [X] rounds of revisions
Additional Revisions: $[Amount] per revision beyond included amount
Approval Process: [Timeframe] for client feedback on each revision
```

#### 6. Cancellation Terms

```
Client Cancellation: [Timeframe] notice required
Refund Policy: [Percentage] of deposit refundable based on work completed
Freelancer Cancellation: [Timeframe] notice required, full deposit refunded
```

### Legal Considerations

#### Common Legal Issues

1. **Scope Creep**: Changes to original agreement
2. **Payment Disputes**: Disagreements over payment amounts or timing
3. **Intellectual Property**: Ownership of work product
4. **Confidentiality**: Protection of client information
5. **Liability**: Responsibility for work errors or damages

#### Protection Strategies

1. **Clear Documentation**: Written agreements for all work
2. **Change Orders**: Formal process for scope changes
3. **Payment Protection**: Deposits and milestone payments
4. **Insurance**: Professional liability insurance
5. **Legal Consultation**: Professional legal review for complex projects
