# AI Job Market Practice Exercises

This section provides hands-on exercises to help you navigate the AI job market, research opportunities, and prepare for your career transition into AI/ML roles.

## Exercise 1: Personal Skills Inventory and Gap Analysis

**Objective:** Conduct a comprehensive self-assessment to identify your current skills and gaps for AI/ML roles.

**Task:** Create a personal skills inventory document.

**Requirements:**

1. List all your technical skills (programming languages, tools, frameworks)
2. List all your soft skills (communication, leadership, problem-solving)
3. Research 5 AI/ML job postings you're interested in
4. Map your skills to job requirements
5. Identify gaps and create a learning roadmap

**Template:**

```markdown
# Personal Skills Inventory - AI/ML Career Transition

## Current Technical Skills
| Skill | Proficiency (1-10) | Years Experience |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|
| Python | 8 | 3 |
| SQL | 6 | 2 |
| Excel | 7 | 5 |

## Current Soft Skills
| Skill | Proficiency (1-10) | Evidence |
|-------|-------------------|----------|
| Communication | 7 | Led team presentations |
| Problem Solving | 8 | Solved complex business issues |

## Target Job Analysis
**Job Title:** Machine Learning Engineer

| Required Skill | My Level | Gap | Action Plan |
|---------------|----------|-----|-------------|
| Python | 8/10 | Small | Portfolio projects |
| TensorFlow | 3/10 | Large | Complete deep learning course |
| Statistics | 5/10 | Medium | Take statistics course |

## Learning Roadmap
1. Month 1-2: Deep learning fundamentals
2. Month 3-4: Build portfolio projects
3. Month 5-6: Network and apply to jobs
```

**Deliverable:** Complete skills inventory with action plan.

## Exercise 8: Salary Negotiation Preparation

**Objective:** Prepare for salary negotiation in AI roles.

**Task:** Research salaries and prepare negotiation strategy.

**Requirements:**

1. Research salary ranges for target roles
2. Calculate your target salary
3. Prepare negotiation scripts
4. Practice negotiation scenarios

**Salary Research Template:**

```markdown
# Salary Negotiation Preparation

## Target Role: Machine Learning Engineer

## Salary Research
| Source | Entry Level | Mid Level | Senior Level |
|--------|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| Glassdoor | $95,000 | $140,000 | $190,000+ |
| Levels.fyi | $100,000 | $155,000 | $210,000+ |
| Indeed | $90,000 | $135,000 | $185,000+ |
| LinkedIn | $95,000 | $145,000 | $200,000+ |

## My Target Salary
- Minimum: $130,000
- Target: $150,000
- Stretch: $175,000

## Total Compensation Breakdown
| Component | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Base Salary | $150,000 |
| Annual Bonus (15%) | $22,500 |
| Stock Options | $30,000 |
| Benefits Value | $15,000 |
| **Total** | **$217,500** |

## Negotiation Talking Points
1. "Based on my research and experience level, I'm targeting $150,000"
2. "I bring [specific skills] that will contribute to [company goals]"
3. "Can you help me understand the full compensation package?"

## If They Lowball
1. "That's lower than my research indicated. Can we discuss?"
2. "What would it take to reach $150,000?"
3. "I need to consider the total package."

## Practice Script
"""Hi [Recruiter], thank you for the offer. I'm excited about the role. 
Based on my research and the value I'll bring, I was expecting a base 
salary of $150,000. Is there flexibility to discuss this?"""
```

**Deliverable:** Complete salary negotiation preparation document.
