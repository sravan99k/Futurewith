## Exercise 1: Identify AI Business Opportunities

**Objective:** Discover and validate AI business opportunities in your target market.

**Task:** Conduct market research to identify 10 viable AI business ideas.

**Requirements:**

1. Analyze problems in industries you're interested in
2. Research existing AI solutions and gaps
3. Evaluate market size and competition
4. Document opportunities with validation plan

**Template:**

```markdown
# AI Business Opportunity Analysis

## Opportunity 1: [Name]
### Problem Statement
[Describe the problem you're solving]

### Target Market
- Industry: [Industry]
- Customer Segment: [B2B/B2C/SMB/Enterprise]
- Market Size: [TAM/SAM/SOM]

### Proposed Solution
[How AI will solve the problem]

### Competitors
| Company | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Competitor A | [What they're good at] | [Their gaps] |
| Competitor B | [What they're good at] | [Their gaps] |

### Unique Value Proposition
[Why customers should choose you]

### Validation Plan
- [ ] Talk to 10 potential customers
- [ ] Build MVP and test
- [ ] Measure willingness to pay
- [ ] Track conversion rates

### Revenue Model
[Subscription/Freemium/Enterprise/Licensing]

### Estimated Timeline
- MVP: [X months]
- First customers: [X months]
- Break-even: [X months]
```

**Deliverable:** Complete analysis of 10 AI business opportunities.

## Exercise 3: AI Pricing Strategy Development

**Objective:** Develop a comprehensive pricing strategy for your AI product.

**Task:** Research, analyze, and implement pricing for your AI business.

**Requirements:**

1. Analyze competitor pricing
2. Calculate unit economics
3. Test pricing models
4. Implement pricing page

**Pricing Analysis Template:**

```markdown
# AI Pricing Strategy: [Product Name]

## Competitor Pricing Analysis
| Competitor | Basic | Pro | Enterprise | Model |
|------------|-------|-----|------------|-------|
| Company A | $29 | $99 | Custom | Per seat |
| Company B | Free | $49 | $499 | Per month |
| Company C | - | $199 | Custom | Usage-based |

## Cost Analysis
### Fixed Costs (Monthly)
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Cloud infrastructure | $500 |
| API subscriptions | $200 |
| Team (if any) | $X |
| Marketing | $300 |

### Variable Costs (Per User)
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| AI API calls | $0.01 |
| Storage | $0.001 |
| Compute | $0.02 |

## Pricing Model Options
### Option A: Subscription (Monthly)
- Free: Limited features
- Pro ($29): Full access
- Enterprise (Custom): White-glove service

### Option B: Usage-Based
- Pay per API call
- Pay per document processed
- Pay per minute of AI processing

### Option C: Hybrid
- Base subscription + usage

## Recommended Strategy
[Choose and justify your pricing model]

## Pricing Page Content
### Headline
"Simple, Transparent Pricing"

### Free Tier
- [Features]
- Perfect for: [Use case]

### Pro Tier - $X/month
- [Features]
- Perfect for: [Use case]
- CTA: "Start Free Trial"

### Enterprise
- [Features]
- Custom pricing
- CTA: "Contact Sales"

## Pricing Psychology
- Annual discount (20%)
- Free trial (7-14 days)
- Price anchoring (show enterprise first)
- Money-back guarantee
```

## Exercise 7: AI Regulatory Compliance Assessment

**Objective:** Understand and address regulatory requirements for AI products.

**Task:** Conduct regulatory analysis for your AI business.

**Requirements:**

1. Identify relevant regulations
2. Assess compliance requirements
3. Create compliance roadmap
4. Implement necessary measures

**Compliance Assessment Template:**

```markdown
# AI Regulatory Compliance Assessment

## Relevant Regulations

### Data Privacy
| Regulation | Region | Key Requirements | Impact |
|------------|--------|------------------|--------|
| GDPR | EU | Consent, data portability, right to delete | High |
| CCPA | California | Disclosure, opt-out, data access | Medium |
| HIPAA | US Healthcare | Health data protection | High (if applicable) |
| LGPD | Brazil | Similar to GDPR | Medium |

### AI-Specific Regulations
| Regulation | Region | Key Requirements | Timeline |
|------------|--------|------------------|----------|
| EU AI Act | EU | Risk classification, transparency | 2024-2026 |
| AI Executive Order | US | Safety standards, reporting | Ongoing |

### Industry-Specific
| Regulation | Industry | Requirements |
|------------|----------|--------------|
| SOC 2 | All SaaS | Security controls |
| PCI DSS | Payments | Card data protection |
| FERPA | Education | Student data privacy |

## Compliance Gap Analysis

| Requirement | Current State | Gap | Action | Priority |
|-------------|--------------|-----|--------|----------|
| Data encryption | Partial | At rest | Implement AES-256 | High |
| Consent management | None | Full | Add consent flows | High |
| Data deletion | None | Full | Build deletion pipeline | Medium |
| Audit logging | Partial | Comprehensive | Expand logging | Medium |

## Compliance Roadmap

### Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Foundation
- [ ] Data inventory
- [ ] Privacy policy update
- [ ] Basic security measures
- [ ] Consent mechanisms

### Phase 2 (Month 3-4): Implementation
- [ ] Encryption at rest
- [ ] Deletion pipelines
- [ ] Access controls
- [ ] Audit logging

### Phase 3 (Month 5-6): Certification
- [ ] SOC 2 preparation
- [ ] Documentation
- [ ] Security audit
- [ ] Certification process

## Budget for Compliance
| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Legal consultation | $5,000 |
| Security tools | $2,000/year |
| Audits | $10,000/year |
| Total Year 1 | ~$17,000 |
```

**Deliverable:** Complete compliance assessment and roadmap.
